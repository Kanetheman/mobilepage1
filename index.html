Our approach

We combine research‑backed modalities with a human, collaborative style. You set the pace—we provide structure, reflection, and skills.

  • Evidence‑based: CBT, DBT‑informed, ACT, EFT, and trauma‑informed care.
  • Whole‑person: Mental health intersects with work, culture, identity, and body.
  • Flexible: In‑person and telehealth, daytime and limited evening hours.
